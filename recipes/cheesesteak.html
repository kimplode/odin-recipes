Philly Cheesesteak Sandwich

Description

In Philadelphia, Pennsylvania there is a debate between two famous shops regarding the best who's Philly Cheesesteak is the best.

Ingredients

  • Oil, for frying
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 24 oz of sliced ribeye
  • Store processed cheese, American or Provolone
  • 4 Italian rolls
  • Sweet green and red peppers, sauteed in oil (optional)
  • Mushrooms sauteed in oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat an iron skillet or a non-stick pan over medium heat and add 3 tablespoons of oil to the pan and saute the onions to desired doneness. Remove the onions
  2. Add the remaining oil and saute the slices of meat quickly on both sides.
  3. If using processed cheese, proceed to melt/warm in a double boiler or in the microwave.
  4. Place 8 ounces of the meat into the rolls, add onions, and pour (or place) the cheese over top.
  5. Garnish with sweet peppers, mushrooms.
