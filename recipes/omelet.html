Wasteland Omelet

Description

Game: Fallout: New Vegas
Available for purchase on The Strip, the Wasteland Omelet is delicious meal for anytime of day. Made of hearty ingredients, the meal is good for the soul and helps heal what ails you. Traditional ingredients include BlamCo Mac & Cheese, Mutfruit, Deathclaw Egg and Lakelurk Meat.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 oz grilled lobster
  • 1/2 box cooked Macaroni & Cheese
  • 1 apple, sliced (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to Medium.
  2. In a medium sized bowl, whisk eggs.
  3. Add remaining ingredients.
  4. Pour egg mixture into bowl.
  5. Allow eggs to cook and set.
  6. Once fully cooked, flip one side onto the other and plate.
  7. Place apple slices atop the omelet.
  8. Enjoy.
