Cranberry Meatball Grinder

Description

Game: Fallout 76

Much easier to prepare with pre-war ingredients, the Cranberry Meatball Grinder could be considered the perfect Thanksgiving meal. Here, we'll substitute the scorchbeast with turkey, a fitting substitution as they both have wings and are an absolute terror.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz premade turkey meatballs
  • 1 sub roll
  • 1 oz mashed cranberries
  • 2 oz mashed potatoes (optional)

Steps

  1. Open up sub roll and spread mashed cranberries along top side.
  2. Spread a layer of mashed potatoes along bottom half of loaf.
  3. Place meatballs between halves.
  4. Bake in oven until warm.
  5. Transfer to plate.
  6. Enjoy.
